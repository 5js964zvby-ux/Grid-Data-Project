THE CHALLENGE

Years of valuable operating history existed, but extracting useful insight took too much manual effort.

Historical records were distributed across raw files with inconsistent naming, formatting, and data quality. Engineers needed a repeatable way to move from scattered source data to trustworthy analysis.
